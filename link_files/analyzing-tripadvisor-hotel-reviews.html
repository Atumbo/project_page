Text Analysis of Tripadvisor Hotel Reviews

This notebook demonstrates simple descriptive analytics techniques for analyzing a significant amount of text data.

Goals:

  • Explore key factors that contribute to hotel satisfaction or dissatisfaction.
  • Use easy-to-understand techniques suitable for communicating with non-technical stakeholders.
  • Provide insights for improving customer satisfaction by diagnosing review patterns over time.

Rating word count

  • Inspect each ratings most frequent words
  • This gives a good indication of what to focus on as a first step in developing operations.
In[25]:
word_counts_rating <- tidy_review2 %>%
    count(word, Rating) %>%
    group_by(Rating) %>%
    top_n(15,n) %>%
    ungroup() %>%  
    mutate(Rating = as.factor(Rating), 
           word = reorder_within(word, n, Rating))
In[26]:

Sentiment analysis

sentiment_rating <- tidy_review2 %>%
    inner_join(get_sentiments("bing"))

sentiment_counts <- sentiment_rating %>%
    count(word, sentiment) %>%
    group_by(sentiment) %>%
    top_n(15,n)%>%
    ungroup() %>%
    mutate(word2 = fct_reorder(word,n))
